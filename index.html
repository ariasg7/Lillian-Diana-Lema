About Me

Design Philosophy

I believe fashion is storytelling, every stitch carries memory, rebellion, and possibility. My work blends the elegance of vintage silhouettes with the daring spirit of avant-garde, creating pieces that feel both timeless and unapologetically modern.

Inspired by music, theatre, and women who turned style into self-expression, I design clothes that move between costume and reality, garments that let people step into their own spotlight.
Fashion, to me, is about liberation. I want my designs to give people the power to feel bold, vulnerable, and completely themselves, like wearing their own story.

Sustainability is at the core of my practice. I focus on zero-waste patterns, upcycled materials, and timeless designs that transcend seasonal trends. Each piece I create tells a story of conscious design and innovative craftsmanship.

Focus Areas

  • • Integration of vintage aesthetics (1950s/60s, 1990s, 2000s) with modern avant-gard
  • • Designing garments that empower authenticity, confidence, and self-expression

  • • Development of cohesive concepts and collections that balance timeless elegance with bold experimentation

Design Process

My systematic approach to fashion design, from initial concept to final garment construction.

01

Research & Inspiration

Gathering inspiration from art, architecture, nature, and cultural movements to inform the design direction.

02
